WHAT PEOPLE

Usually Asked

What services does ProHealth offer?

We offer general consultations, pediatrics, cardiology, neurology, obstetrics & gynecology, psychiatry, and emergency care.

How do I schedule an appointment?

Use the appointment form below or call our support line. Choose a department, date, and time that works for you.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes. We work with major providers. Billing staff can help verify coverage before your visit.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), current medications list, and prior medical records if available.
